Real-Time Flyer Deals IntegrationOptiDeals integrates real-time flyer deals from nearby Ontario grocers. This allows users to customize their weekly meals based on the latest deals and prices, ensuring they get the best value for their money.
AI-Powered Meal PlanningLeveraging OpenAI’s advanced algorithms, OptiDeals generates personalized meal plans based on user preferences and current deals. This eliminates the need for manual searching and planning, saving users time and effort.
Budget-Friendly Recipe GenerationOptiDeals takes savings to the next level by allowing users to set their budget within the app. The AI then curates a week’s worth of delicious recipes based on the best flyer deals available, making it possible to enjoy diverse and tasty meals without breaking the bank.
